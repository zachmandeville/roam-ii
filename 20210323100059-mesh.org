#+title: mesh

A group of hosts that coordinate to provide a consistent network topology.

In the documentation, an "Envoy Mesh"  is a group of [[file:20210216102259-envoy_proxy.org][Envoy Proxies]] that provide a message passing substrated for a distributed system of many different services and application platforms.
